EXPEDITED SHIPPING SERVICES
Trinity Logistics arranges expedited shipping via both road and air.

When less-than-truckload (LTL) shipping isn’t quick enough, or doesn’t provide the special handling your shipment requires, Trinity Logistics can arrange expedited and specialty transportation services to get the job done. We’d love to be your one-stop shop for all logistics solutions, including shipments that require special attention.

Over-the-Road (OTR)

If you need your shipment moved faster than standard LTL transit times allow, we offer expedited shipping options for shipments between one and twelve pallets that weigh under 10,000 pounds. Utilizing carriers with right-sized vehicles, including cargo vans, sprinters and straight trucks, we can arrange for delivery on a tight turn-around – for example, tradeshows or short appointment windows.

Air

When expedited OTR isn’t fast enough, we can arrange door-to-door transport of your freight via air.

Hotshot and Dedicated Shipping

Sometimes having your freight on its own vehicle without multiple stops is best, whether the freight is time-sensitive, unusually fragile, or both. We can arrange hotshot/dedicated truck options to get your freight on a single vehicle and to its destination without stops at terminals in between.
