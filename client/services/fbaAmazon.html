CANADA & MEXICO SHIPPING

International shipping is far more complicated than domestic shipping. Relying on an experienced logistics provider like Trinity Logistics is imperative to ensure your imports and exports cross the border smoothly. Trinity Logistics is a qualified provider in these ways:

All Modes Of Transportation

Whether you need to ship LTL, full truckload, door-to-door intermodal, or a combination of modes, Trinity can arrange the transportation for it all! Don’t complicate your Mexico shipments by involving multiple companies, carriers and logistics service providers. Keep it simple and make a single call, every single time.

Experience

With over 20 years of experience in this area of the industry and multiple office locations in border cities, you can trust Trinity to have the knowledge and expertise required for things to go smoothly when you’re shipping to Mexico.

24/7 Communication

Trinity offers you a constant flow of information from origin to destination, including communications on your behalf with customs clearance, brokerage checkpoints, and the motor carrier. We can even prepare detailed letters of instruction on your behalf. Every carrier we select has tracking/tracing methods in place, and many supply satellite access. Your freight won’t “vanish” once it crosses the border, which is a common problem with other providers. We keep full visibility throughout the process.

Bilingual Offices

Fluent Spanish-speaking Trinity team members with extensive knowledge of Mexico and its shipping procedures work closely with our providers to ensure nothing is lost in translation.

Flexibility

We know that every customer has specific preferences and requirements when their freight is transported between Mexico and the U.S. Depending on your needs, we can arrange a door-to-door movement on the same trailer (so the cargo only changes hands, not trailers), a transload, or a warehouse unload. Our solutions are as flexible as your situation requires.
