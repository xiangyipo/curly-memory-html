OUR GUIDING VALUES
Learn about the core values that make up Trinity Logistics.

At SZCTD Logistics, our company culture revolves around a set of guiding values that our team members live by every single day. SZCTD’s concept is “Technology innovates service; Professionalism creates efficiency”. We value knowledge creative & innovative and combine latest information technology with the good experience from traditional logistics industry. We have a professional IT team with rich experience & excellent skills in our company.

Integrity: We do the right thing! Determination: We are persistent and unshakable in overcoming obstacles. Continuous Improvement: We aspire to reach our {untapped, full, greatest} potential. Teamwork: We listen. We collaborate. We solve. Fun: Fun lives here! Excellence: We challenge the status quo.
